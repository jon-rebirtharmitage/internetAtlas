Internet Atlas

Discover more about your interwebs then just the pipes.

How to Use

To use this site simply click on the Add Address button to add in locations you want to check on available internet speeds. Then once you have entered in all the ones you want (up to 9 at a time) then you click Compare and it will compare and load the results for you. This will work with only 1 address if you only want to check the one as well.

About

This is a pet project that I started after reading an Arstechnica article about a man in California who had to move from his home because the internet he could get could not support his remote work based job. Since I would never move somewhere without internet speeds capable of running all the internets I figured why should anybody else. So if you are looking to move and you want to make sure you don't have to sacrifice your Call of Duty link rate to someone else in the house playing on Netflix then use this site to insure the internet you need is where you are going.
